Michael Pisani/Benevolence Messiah

Michael Pisani, also known as Benevolence Messiah, is a Maltese-Irish polymath born in Detroit, Michigan, known for his extensive knowledge and expertise in a multitude of disciplines. Trained in the art of political and geographical analysis, comparative geopolitical economy, and dialectical analysis, he has made inroads in the burgeoning fields of music art, and technology. As a musician and artist, his work is characterized by a unique blend of genres and extensive experimentation. Benevolence Messiah's discography, comprised of nearly 800 tracks, totaling 1.5TB of data. His musical projects span a wide range of genres such as Psydub, LoFi, Triphop, Aramaic, and more, all united by an overarching psychedelic theme that transcends traditional boundaries. In addition to his musical pursuits, Benevolence Messiah is a visual artist, proficient in 3D and 2D modeling, animations, AI visual, video, and image generation art, Snapchat lenses, AR art, and multi-sensory art. He has developed several AI web UI AI inferences for the democratization of AI and hosts around 100 repositories, web apps/spaces, and AI projects on GitHub and HuggingFace.

Pisani's work as a political philosopher is equally impressive. He is well-versed in various forms of Comparative government and economics.
